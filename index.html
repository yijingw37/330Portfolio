VR Third Place Project

This is a VR project that offers users a virtual third place. My starting idea is to have a pink universe for people to hang out. I tried to build a world with a couple pink planets in it, and a puppy. I want my guests to feel relaxed. I also added an audio of nature as background music. My room has mystery and enticement. The planets and rainbow in my room are the mystery items that you don't see in everyday life and contribute to the mysterious atmosphere in the room.
