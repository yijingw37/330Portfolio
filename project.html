VR Social Space Project

I took COMP_SCI 396 Social AR/VR last quarter. The course was focused on social space and interaction through AR/VR technology. I made a VR social space where you can hang out with your friends in Glitch. I have embedded my Glitch project in this page. Please use your mouse to change direction. Please use your keyboard to walk around. Try to interact with the objects in this space using the circle cursor. Please note: not every object is interactive and I will leave it to you to figure out what is interactive.
